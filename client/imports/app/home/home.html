About us

Hip Samba is a 3-day event that is scheduled to take place on May 1st, May 27th & May 29th 2018 in Barnawa Kaduna. Villageboi Communications & Technology, producers of the television show “MEMO” and Independent producers for AREWA 24 will produce and host this event. The purpose of the event is to Showcase the best of Nigerian contemporary and traditional music, culture and art under one umbrella and intellectually stimulate discussions intended to re-awaken the intrinsic cultural people’s pride and prestige. The festival will consist of 3 days of headlining entertainment at Space 2000, located in Barnawa Kaduna with activities including concert performances, cultural troupes display, food, drinks, games, arts, marketplace, entrepreneurial activities and more for attendees to enjoy The festival is expected to attract more than 5000 people onsite for the 3 events and about 10,000 through live streaming.


Festival Objectives

To imbibe in young Nigerians strong values of self-reliance, honesty, industry, thrift, enterprise and a culture of excellence

Create an event that will rise to be the country’s most vibrant celebration of cultural diversity, colour and sound

To strengthen the bond uniting Nigerians and portray positively the country and its people

To Combine traditional and modern contemporary sound and dance that will appeal to the widest audience in terms of age and geographical spread.


Why Hip Samba

Nigeria is a multiethnic composition with rich and diverse cultural heritage that has been ravaged by series of ethno-religious crises and its evident that there is a growing disconnect between the ethnicities of Nigeria. Also, Nigeria is yet to fully explore the potentials of its ethnic diversity in this vast world in which uniqueness is an adornment. Hip Samba pursues to serve as a probable means of curtailing this irregularity, by creatively fusing education and entertainment in one event that will bring these ethnicities together there by creating a vibrant mix of music, culture, dance, arts and entrepreneurial activities all while promoting and celebrating local ingenuity.
